About Ethereum Classic (ETC)

Ethereum Classic (ETC) is the original Ethereum blockchain, which came into existence after a split following the DAO hack in 2016. It continues to run without any changes to the protocol, emphasizing immutability and decentralization. Ethereum Classic allows developers to build decentralized applications (dApps) using smart contracts.

  • ETC is focused on maintaining the immutability of the blockchain.
  • It is used to power decentralized applications and smart contracts.
  • ETC is supported by a dedicated community of developers and miners.

Help

Connect your Ethereum Classic wallet. Make sure you have a wallet compatible with the ETC network (e.g., MetaMask, Trust Wallet).
Complete the tasks on the official page, including actions on social media or confirming participation through the form.
After completing the tasks, you will automatically receive your tokens. You can track the distribution status on your account page.

You can use the Ethereum Classic Block Explorer, such as BlockScout, for this. Enter your wallet address in the search field to view your balance and transactions.

Smart contracts are programs that automatically execute code when certain conditions are met. Ethereum Classic supports smart contracts for decentralized applications (dApps).
